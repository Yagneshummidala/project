Weekly Meal Plan For Normal Patients

Sunday

Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM)

2 whole eggs (scrambled or boiled) + 1 toasted whole wheat bread + 1/2 cup low-fat milk (no sugar)

Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM)

1 cup papaya

Lunch (2:00-2:30PM)

1 cup arhar dal (pigeon pea lentils) + 2 chapatis (whole wheat) + 1/2 cup low-fat curd + Salad with various veggies

Evening (4:00-4:30PM)

1 cup vegetable soup (low-sodium)

Dinner (8:00-8:30PM)

1 cup pumpkin curry + 2 chapatis + Salad with cucumber and tomatoes

Monday

Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM)

1 onion stuffed chapatti (use minimal oil) + 1/2 cup low-fat curd

Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM)

1 cup coconut water

Lunch (2:00-2:30PM)

1 cup moong dal (mung bean lentils) or lean protein source like grilled chicken+ 2 chapatis + Salad with various veggies

Evening (4:00-4:30PM)

1 cup pomegranate

Dinner (8:00-8:30PM)

1 cup beans curry + 2 chapatis + Salad with cucumber and bell peppers

Tuesday

Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM)

2 besan cheela (gram flour pancakes) with veggies + 1/2 cup low+fat curd

Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM)

1 apple

Lunch (2:00-2:30PM)

1 cup masoor dal (red lentils) + 2 chapatis + 1/2 cup low+fat curd + Salad with various veggies

Evening (4:00-4:30PM)

1 cup tomato soup (low - sodium)

Dinner (8:00-8:30PM)

1 cup carrot and peas vegetable + 2 chapatis + Salad with cucumber and bell peppers

Wednesday

Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM)

1 cup vegetable brown bread upma (use minimal oil) + 1/2 cup low-fat milk (no sugar)

Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM)

1 cup musk melon

Lunch (2:00-2:30PM)

1 cup rajma (kidney bean) curry + 2 chapatis + Salad with various veggies

Evening (4:00-4:30PM)

1 cup vegetable soup

Dinner (8:00-8:30PM)

1 cup parwal (pointed gourd) vegetable + 2 chapatis + Salad with cucumber and bell peppers

Thursday

Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM)

1 cucumber hung curd sandwich with a light chutney + 1 orange

Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM)

1 cup buttermilk

Lunch (2:00-2:30PM)

1 cup white chana/ fish curry + 3 chapatti + 1/2 cup rice + salad

Evening (4:00-4:30PM)

1 cup sprouts salad

Dinner (8:00-8:30PM)

1 cup cauliflower vegetable + 1 chapatti + salad

Friday

Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM)

1 cup vegetable poha + 1 cup low fat curd

Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM)

1 cup watermelon

Lunch (2:00-2:30PM)

1 cup chana dal + 1 chapatti + salad

Evening (4:00-4:30PM)

1 cup sprouts salad

Dinner (8:00-8:30PM)

1 cup tinda vegetable + 1 chapatti + salad

Saturday

Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM)

1 cup low fat milk with oats + 3-4 strawberries

Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM)

1 cup coconut water

Lunch (2:00-2:30PM)

1 cup soybean curry + 1 chapatti + 1/2 cup low fat curd + salad

Evening (4:00-4:30PM)

1 cup fruit salad

Dinner (8:00-8:30PM)

1 cup ghia vegetable + 1 chaptti + salad
